foilgroup.toc

foilgroup.toc — Put ToC on foilgroup pages?

Synopsis

<xsl:param name="foilgroup.toc" select="1"></xsl:param>

Description

If non-zero, a ToC will be placed on foilgroup pages (after any other content).
